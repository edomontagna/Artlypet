# Vision — Artlypet

## Problem
Pet owners want unique, artistic representations of their beloved animals but custom pet portraits are expensive (€100-500+), slow (weeks), and require finding a skilled artist. The €200B+ global pet care market is booming, with personalized products as the fastest-growing segment.

## Solution
Artlypet uses AI (Gemini Nano Banana 2) to transform any pet photo into a stunning artistic portrait in seconds. Museum-quality digital art at a fraction of the cost, with optional physical prints shipped EU-wide.

## Target User
- **Primary**: Pet owners aged 25-55, EU-based, digitally comfortable, active on Instagram/TikTok
- **Secondary**: Gift buyers — birthdays, Christmas, adoption celebrations, memorials
- **Tertiary**: Social media enthusiasts wanting shareable pet content
- **B2B**: Pet shops, veterinary clinics, pet e-commerce — white-label platform

## Value Proposition
- **Free to start**: 300 credits on signup (3 single portraits free)
- **Instant**: Results in under 60 seconds
- **12 art styles**: Royal, Oil Painting, Watercolor, Pop Art, Anime, Military, Victorian, and more
- **HD quality**: Full 2K resolution for premium users
- **Print ready**: Museum-quality canvas prints from €59.90
- **Mix mode**: Pet + person together in one portrait

## Competitive Landscape
- **Crown & Paw**: $10M+ revenue, manual artists, $50-150/portrait, 3-5 day delivery
- **Pawtist**: AI-based, limited styles, no freemium
- **Our edge**: Freemium model + instant delivery + 12 styles + white-label B2B

## Growth Strategy
1. **Viral loop**: Free tier → watermarked images shared on social → brand exposure → conversions
2. **Gift economy**: Seasonal spikes (Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's Day)
3. **B2B partnerships**: Pet shops embed Artlypet as value-add service
4. **Content marketing**: Instagram/TikTok with before/after pet portraits
5. **Referral program**: Give 10%, get 10% (planned)
